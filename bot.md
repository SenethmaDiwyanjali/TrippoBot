Your project Trippo Bot is an AI-powered travel recommendation website designed to help travelers quickly find suitable destinations, accommodation, and activities based on their personal preferences.

In this system, the user first provides several inputs such as travel group type (friends, family, couple, or solo), number of travel days, budget level, and personal interests such as adventure, nature, or relaxation. After receiving these inputs, the AI system analyzes the information and compares it with a small travel dataset containing destinations, activities, hotels, and restaurants.

Based on the user’s preferences, the system generates personalized travel recommendations. These recommendations include suitable destinations, activities that match the user’s interests, affordable hotels that match the user’s budget, and nearby restaurants. For example, if a user plans to travel with friends for two days with a low budget and prefers adventure activities, the system may recommend locations such as hiking spots, affordable guest houses, and budget-friendly food places suitable for short trips.

Inputs:
Travel Group(Family,Friends,Solo,couple)
Number of Days
Budget(Budget,Moderate,Luxury)
Interest (Adventure, Nature, Relaxation, Culture,Food,Wildlife,Beach,History)


Outputs:
Suggested destinations
Activities to do (Hiking,Beach,etc)
Hotels to stay
Restaurants to eat
Best Season
Rating
